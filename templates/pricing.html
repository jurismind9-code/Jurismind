Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I reach my document limit?
When you reach your monthly document limit, you'll need to upgrade to Pro or wait for the next billing cycle. Your existing documents and analyses remain accessible.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your billing period.
What file formats are supported?
Free plan supports PDF files only. Pro plan supports PDF, DOCX, TXT files, and can also load content from URLs.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take security seriously. Your documents are processed securely and are not stored permanently. We use industry-standard encryption for all data transfers.
